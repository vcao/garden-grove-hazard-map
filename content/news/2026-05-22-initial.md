---
title: Incident declared at GKN Aerospace
date: "2026-05-22"
summary: Hazmat response underway after a tank of methyl methacrylate began leaking at the GKN facility.
---

Garden Grove emergency management has declared a hazardous materials incident at the **GKN Aerospace** facility. A storage tank containing **methyl methacrylate (MMA)** has begun leaking and an evacuation zone has been established:

- **North:** Ball Road
- **South:** Trask Avenue
- **East:** Dale Street
- **West:** Valley View Street

Residents within the highlighted area should leave immediately and proceed to the nearest evacuation or reunification center listed in the **Centers** tab.

This page will be updated as new information becomes available. For official communications, follow the [OCFA feed](#) and check the [About MMA](#) tab for chemical safety information.
